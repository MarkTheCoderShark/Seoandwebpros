Custom Design FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our custom website design services.

Why choose custom design over templates?

Custom design ensures your website stands out from competitors, perfectly matches your brand, and is optimized for your specific goals. While templates can be a quick solution, custom design provides unique features, better performance, and higher conversion rates.

How long does custom design take?

A custom website typically takes 6-12 weeks from concept to launch, depending on complexity. This includes discovery (1-2 weeks), design (2-4 weeks), development (2-4 weeks), and testing/refinement (1-2 weeks). We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We build custom websites with user-friendly content management systems that allow you to easily update content, add pages, and manage media. We provide training and documentation, plus ongoing support if needed.

What's included in custom design?

Our custom design service includes brand analysis, user experience design, responsive development, SEO optimization, custom functionality, content management system, analytics integration, and training. We also provide post-launch support and optimization.
