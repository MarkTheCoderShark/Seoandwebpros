Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our custom website design services.

What makes custom design different from templates?

Custom design means every element is created specifically for your brand and business needs. Unlike templates, you get unlimited design possibilities, unique functionality, and a website that truly reflects your brand identity without any limitations.

How long does custom website design take?

Custom projects typically take 8-16 weeks depending on complexity and scope. This includes discovery, design concepts, revisions, development, testing, and launch. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Will I own my custom website design?

Yes, absolutely! Upon final payment, you own all rights to your custom design, code, and content. We provide all source files and documentation, giving you complete ownership and control over your website.

Can you integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, we specialize in custom integrations with CRM systems, payment processors, inventory management, email marketing platforms, and virtually any API-based service your business uses.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Since we built your website, we're the best equipped to maintain it.
