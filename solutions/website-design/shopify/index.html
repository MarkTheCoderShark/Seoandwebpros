Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Shopify design and development services.

Why choose Shopify for my e-commerce store?

Shopify is the leading e-commerce platform powering over 1.7 million businesses worldwide. It offers secure hosting, mobile optimization, built-in SEO features, and seamless payment processing, making it perfect for businesses of all sizes.

How long does it take to design a Shopify store?

A basic Shopify store typically takes 2-4 weeks, while custom stores with advanced features can take 6-10 weeks. The timeline depends on complexity, number of products, and custom functionality requirements.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify?

Yes! We specialize in Shopify migrations from platforms like WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and others. We ensure all products, customer data, and SEO rankings are preserved during the migration.

Will my Shopify store be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our Shopify stores are designed with a mobile-first approach, ensuring perfect functionality and appearance across all devices. Mobile optimization is crucial since over 70% of e-commerce traffic comes from mobile devices.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive Shopify maintenance packages including security updates, app updates, performance monitoring, backup management, and technical support to keep your store running smoothly.
