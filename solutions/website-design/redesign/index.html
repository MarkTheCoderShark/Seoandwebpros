Website Redesign FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our website redesign services.

How long does a website redesign take?

A typical website redesign takes 8-12 weeks, depending on complexity. This includes analysis (1-2 weeks), design (2-3 weeks), development (3-4 weeks), and testing/migration (2-3 weeks). We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Will I lose my search rankings?

No! We carefully preserve your SEO value during redesign. This includes maintaining URL structures, setting up proper redirects, preserving meta data, and ensuring content continuity. Most clients see improved rankings after redesign due to better performance and user experience.

What happens to my existing content?

We carefully migrate all your valuable content to the new design. During the process, we can also help optimize and reorganize content for better impact. You'll have the opportunity to review and approve all content changes before launch.

Will there be any downtime?

No! We build and test your new website on a staging server while your current site remains live. When ready, we perform the switch during off-peak hours, typically with less than 5 minutes of transition time.
