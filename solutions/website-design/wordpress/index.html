Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our WordPress website design services.

Why choose WordPress for my website?

WordPress powers over 40% of all websites globally because it's flexible, user-friendly, and SEO-friendly. It offers thousands of themes and plugins, making it perfect for any type of website from blogs to e-commerce stores.

How long does WordPress development take?

A basic WordPress site typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex sites with custom functionality can take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements during our consultation.

Will I be able to update my WordPress site myself?

Absolutely! WordPress has an intuitive dashboard that makes content management easy. We provide training and documentation to help you confidently manage your site, and we're always available for support.

Do you provide WordPress maintenance services?

Yes! We offer comprehensive WordPress maintenance packages including security updates, plugin updates, backups, performance monitoring, and technical support to keep your site secure and running optimally.

Can you migrate my existing site to WordPress?

Yes, we specialize in WordPress migrations from any platform. We ensure all your content, SEO rankings, and functionality are preserved during the migration process with minimal to no downtime.
