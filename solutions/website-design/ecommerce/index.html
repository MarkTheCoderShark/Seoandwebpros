E-commerce Design FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our e-commerce website design services.

Which e-commerce platform should I choose?

We'll help you choose the best platform based on your needs, whether it's Shopify, WooCommerce, or a custom solution. Each platform has its strengths, and we'll recommend the one that best fits your business goals, budget, and technical requirements.

How long does it take to build an e-commerce site?

The timeline varies based on complexity and features. A basic e-commerce site typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex stores with custom functionality can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

What payment methods can I accept?

We can integrate any major payment gateway including Stripe, PayPal, Square, and more. You can accept credit cards, digital wallets, and alternative payment methods. We'll help you choose the best options for your target market.

Can you help with product setup and migration?

Yes! We offer complete product setup services including bulk import, category organization, and product optimization. If you're migrating from another platform, we'll ensure all your products, customer data, and order history are transferred correctly.
