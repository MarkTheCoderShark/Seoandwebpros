Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mobile app development services and process.

How long does mobile app development take?

Development timelines vary based on app complexity. A simple app typically takes 3-4 months, while complex apps with advanced features can take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Should I choose native or cross-platform development?

The choice depends on your specific needs. Native apps offer optimal performance and platform-specific features, while cross-platform apps provide cost efficiency and faster development. We'll recommend the best approach based on your budget, timeline, and requirements.

Do you handle App Store submissions?

Yes! We handle the complete App Store and Google Play Store submission process, including creating store listings, preparing screenshots, writing descriptions, and managing the approval process to ensure successful app launches.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, OS updates, feature enhancements, performance monitoring, and technical support. Our maintenance packages ensure your app stays current and performs optimally.
