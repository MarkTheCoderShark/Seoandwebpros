Development FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our web development services.

How long does web development take?

Development timelines vary based on project complexity. A basic website typically takes 4-8 weeks, while complex web applications can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation based on your specific requirements.

What technology stack do you recommend?

We recommend technology stacks based on your specific needs, scalability requirements, and budget. Common stacks include MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) for modern web apps, PHP/Laravel for enterprise solutions, and specialized platforms like WordPress or Shopify for specific use cases.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. We ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally.

How do you ensure security?

Security is built into every stage of our development process. We implement SSL certificates, secure coding practices, regular security audits, automated backups, and follow OWASP security guidelines. We also provide regular security updates and monitoring.
