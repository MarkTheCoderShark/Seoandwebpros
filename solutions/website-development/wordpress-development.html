Frequently Asked Questions About WordPress Development

Get answers to common questions about our WordPress development services and process.

How long does it take to develop a custom WordPress website?

The timeline for WordPress development varies based on complexity and requirements. A simple business website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom functionality projects can take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the process.

What's the difference between WordPress.com and WordPress.org?

WordPress.org is the self-hosted version that gives you complete control and ownership of your website. This is what we use for all our custom development projects. WordPress.com is a hosted service with limitations on customization and plugins. We always recommend WordPress.org for businesses seeking full control and scalability.

Do you provide ongoing WordPress maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive WordPress maintenance packages including security updates, performance optimization, regular backups, uptime monitoring, and technical support. Our maintenance services ensure your website remains secure, fast, and up-to-date with the latest WordPress versions.

Can you migrate my existing website to WordPress?

Absolutely! We specialize in migrating websites from various platforms including Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, custom HTML sites, and other CMS platforms to WordPress. Our migration process ensures zero data loss, maintains SEO rankings, and includes performance optimization.

Is WordPress secure for business websites?

When properly configured and maintained, WordPress is highly secure. We implement multiple security layers including SSL certificates, security plugins, regular updates, strong authentication, and security monitoring. Our WordPress sites follow industry best practices for security and compliance.

What's included in your WordPress development service?

Our WordPress development includes custom theme development, plugin integration, responsive design, SEO optimization, performance optimization, security setup, content migration, training, and post-launch support. We provide a complete solution from concept to launch and beyond.
